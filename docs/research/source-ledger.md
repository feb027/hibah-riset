## Immediate citation strategy

1. **Do not anchor novelty on YOLO26 alone.** YOLO26 is useful, but current academic anchor should be YOLOv10/RT-DETR/D-FINE/DEIM/RF-DETR for NMS-free/end-to-end real-time detection.
2. **Use DiffMOT as the central tracker novelty source** and compare it with DiffusionTrack, OC-SORT, Hybrid-SORT, BoostTrack, DragonTrack, OcclusionTrack, and 2025/2026 occlusion-aware MOT.
3. **Separate people counting paradigms**: density-map crowd counting vs detection-tracking-counting. The proposal is the second; crowd-counting surveys support background but not enough for trajectory/ID logic.
4. **Use vendor docs only for implementation rationale**: Ultralytics YOLO26 and NVIDIA DeepStream can justify feasibility/architecture, not academic novelty.
5. **Keep canonical older sources** for datasets/metrics only: MOT20, DanceTrack, CrowdHuman, HOTA, IDF1.
